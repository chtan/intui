#### Read Information and Deduce

<br>

It is given that

- $|A| = 75\%$
- $|B| = 70\%$
- $|C| = 65\%$

This implies that

- $|A^c| = 25\%$
- $|B^c| = 30\%$
- $|C^c| = 35\%$

Here the notation $^c$ refers to the set-theoretic complement. Thus, for example, $A^c$ comprises the students who do not play badminton.
